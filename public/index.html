Welcome to Campus Plate


Campus Plate is a platform that allows users to recover food at various Food Stops around campus. This project is sponsored by the Environmental Protection Agency(EPA)'s P3 program with the primary goal of reducing food waste. Dr. Franklin Lebo and Dr. Brian Krupp are the principal investigators of this project.

If you are part of the pilot, but did not receive an email to install the application, please email Dr. Brian Krupp at bkrupp@bw.edu. If you are a Food Stop manager, please click here to access your Food Stop. If you are just interested in the project, you can email Dr. Franklin Lebo or Dr. Brian Krupp
